Go to a .spacemacs variable with helm-spacemacs

August 20, 2015

You can quickly go to any variable in your .spacemacs by invoking helm-spacemacs with SPC f e h. In the example below, we navigate to spacemacs-editing-style by searching for “style”. You can also use this tool to navigate to the READMEs for any layer, the spacemacs documentation, and toggle options.
