Refocus Helm

October 20, 2015

Sometimes when Helm loses focus, it breaks a little bit (try clicking outside the helm buffer to see what I mean.) If this happens, you’ve got two options:

  1. start another helm session (which will kill the existing buffer)
  2. use switch-to-minibuffer-window (SPC w b) to refocus your session
